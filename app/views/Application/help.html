How to request for a Session

Widgr is an easy and comfortable way to learn skills and get help from experts over the Internet. No matter who you are, where you are, or what time it is, you can talk to someone who can help you.

Widgr is a place where you can find people offering help on a wide variety of subjects. We call them Widgr Publishers.

This video example shows you how to search and request a session from a Publisher.



How to request for Publisher account

Ever wanted to share cooking recipes, speak a new language, or provide health tips? Widgr allows you to share your passion and expertise through live video, right from your computer or mobile device

For becoming a Publisher you will need fill additional personal information about yourself, such as your education and experiences to ensure people that you are the right person they are searching for.

Next step is to create your first channel. After you submit a channel, it will go through a review process to ensure it complies with Widgr Policies. Once your channel is reviewed, we will send you a notification and let you know whether channel was approved.

This video example shows you how to request for a Publisher account.



How to set up your Publisher account for PayPal

When you decide to start providing paid Channels through Widgr, all you need is to enter your PayPal account name in Settings page. It must be a verified Premier or Business account, as you will use this account for receiving money.

This video example shows you where you can set up your PayPal account.



How to connect your account with Facebook

You can use Facebook to speed up registration process and afterwards you will be able to log into Widgr with your facebook account. You can always change or disconnect Widgr from your Facebook account anytime

As a Widgr Publisher you might find useful another feature. If you own a Facebook page, you can install Widgr extension to your Facebook page. With this option your Widgr profile will be displayed on a custom page tab within your Facebook page. So it will become more accessible to your existing Facebook customers

This video example shows how connect your Facebook account and add widgr to your Facebook page



How to sync your sessions with Google Calendar

For better planning you might find useful another feature. You can connect Widgr with Google Calendar. With this option you can be notified about upcoming Sessions on every device where you are using Google Calendar

This video example shows how to sync your Sessions with Google Calendar
